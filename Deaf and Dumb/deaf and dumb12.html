Science Experiments

Mixing Lemon and Baking Soda in Water

A simple science experiment where mixing lemon juice with baking soda in water creates a chemical reaction, producing carbon dioxide gas and causing fizzing.

Bouncy Egg

A science experiment where soaking an egg in vinegar dissolves the shell, leaving behind a rubbery, bouncy egg due to the reaction between the acid and calcium carbonate.

Water rises in a bottle

In this experiment, placing a lit candle inside a bottle and covering it with water causes the flame to extinguish, creating a partial vacuum that draws the water upward into the bottle.
